French Omelette

Description:

A silky smooth omelette slowly cooked with butter

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 knobs of butter
  • Salt
  • Time
  • Heat

Steps:

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl
  2. With two spoons, pinch off the white stuff attached to the egg
  3. Whisk the eggs until they fall off the whisk like water
  4. Heat a pan on low and add a knob of butter to the pan
  5. Add eggs and slowly cook them for 10 mins, making sure to constantly be stirring them
  6. Allow the eggs to form a skin on the bottom of the pan with the top still being wet
  7. With a soft spoon or spatula start on one side of the pan and roll the egg onto itself
  8. Serve on a plate, take some butter and rub in on the omelette to make it shiny, and garnish with green onions
