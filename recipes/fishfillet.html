Fish Fillet

Description:

A fillet from a fish that is pan seared or baked in an oven. Often cooked with either butter or oil garnished with a green herb like dill.

Ingredients:

  • Fish; salmon, trout, char...
  • Butter or Oil
  • Salt
  • Time
  • Heat

Steps:

  1. Score the fish with alternating lines with a knife
  2. Salt the top liberally
  3. Put flesh side down into hot pan
  4. Cook until flesh side has formed a crust and flip to skin side
  5. Cook skin until desired crispyness
  6. Finish cooking once internal temp reaches 145 degrees
