Spam and Fried Rice

Description:

Rice fried with spam and eggs and veggies.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • Can of spam
  • Jasmine rice
  • Peas (optional)
  • Tamari

Steps:

  1. Start preparing your rice with your preffered method
  2. Slice the spam into little cubes and brown in pan
  3. Once the spam is browned and crispy add in cooked rice and peas
  4. Fry for a little bit, then add 3 eggs and mix well
  5. Season with tamari and salt to taste then serve
